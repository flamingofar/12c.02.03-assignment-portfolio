Fonts and UI-Cards

This project is about fonts, typescaling and UI cards. Here i went through font pairing, visiual hierachy and displaying information nice as a ui card.

Fonts and font pairing is not a easy task. It is all based upon what you want to communicate, and what the tone of voice on the website should be. On this particular project i went with, Abril Fatface as the heading font and Raleway as text font. These two complement each other, in the sense of contrast. The heading display font is bold and extravagant and the text font is simple and elegant.

Fonts and type scaling

As i got futher into fonts and typefaces, i had to implement them on the site. Here i utilized a type scale tool to get the right proportions of the scale of the fonts. Here i went with a Major Third relation, as this gives a nice and fluid segway throughout the font sizes.
